Common questions

How do I log something?
Tap "Log a go-to" from any screen, choose whether it's a place or a thing, pick a category, and add your rating and notes.

Who can see what I log?
Only you. go-to. is private by default, with no feeds, followers, or public profiles.

How do I delete my account?
Go to the Profile screen and choose Delete account. This removes all your logged data and your profile.
