Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

This is your data. We treat it that way.

What we collect

When you sign in to go-to. with Apple or Google, we receive your email address and (if you allow it) your name. We use these only to identify your account and personalise your experience.

When you log a go-to, we store what you enter: the place or thing, your rating, your notes, and the categories you assign. This data is stored on Supabase, the database service that powers the app.

We do not collect your location, your contacts, your photos, or anything you haven't typed in yourself.

What we don't do

We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. We don't run analytics tools that track you across other apps or websites. We don't show ads.

Your logged go-tos are visible only to you. There are no social features, no shared lists, no public profiles.

Who can see your data

You, on your devices.

The app's developer has technical access to the database, but only for two specific purposes: auditing for security and abuse, and troubleshooting bugs or errors you report. We don't read individual users' logs out of curiosity, and we never share or publish them.

Supabase, our database provider, stores your data on their secure infrastructure. They have their own privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.

Apple and Google, depending on which one you sign in with, handle the authentication itself. We never see your password.

Deleting your account

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen. Doing so removes all your logged data and your profile from our systems.

Children

go-to. is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy materially, we'll update the "last updated" date and (where appropriate) notify you in the app.

Contact

Questions about your data, or this policy? Email kevockdigital@gmail.com.
